## 🙋 About Me

<table>
<tr>
<td valign="top" width="58%">

I build products around a few recurring themes: **agentdocs-orchestrator** for multi-agent orchestration and AI workflows, **one-id** for OAuth 2.1 / OpenID Connect identity, **FluxCaption** for subtitle generation and translation, **mini-web** for browser-based remote access, and **TgGod** for Telegram media automation and archiving.

Together, these projects reflect the areas I care about most: **AI workflows, identity infrastructure, self-hosted platforms, automation tooling, and networking-oriented systems**. My main stack is **.NET, TypeScript, Python, and Go**, and I prefer building tools that can survive real production use.
